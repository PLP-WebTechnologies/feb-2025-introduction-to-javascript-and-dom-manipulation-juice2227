About Us

HerZone254 is a youth-led initiative focused on ending femicide and GBV through awareness, education, and empowerment.
HerZone254 started with a question: What can we do to protect women and girls in our own communities? We're a group of young creators, coders, poets, and changemakers using our voices to stand against GBV and femicide.

Therefore, our work encompasses:

  1. Providing Essential Services: We offer comprehensive support, including counseling, legal aid, and practical resources, tailored to the unique needs of each survivor.
  2. Creating Safe Spaces: We establish secure and supportive environments where survivors can find refuge and begin their journey towards recovery.
  3. Advocating for Systemic Change: We actively challenge the deep-seated patriarchal structures that perpetuate violence, pushing for policy reforms and societal shifts that promote equality and justice.

What is GBV & Femicide?

Learn about the different types of gender-based violence and how femicide affects women and girls across Kenya.
